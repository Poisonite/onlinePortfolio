Quick Mobile Repair

These devices included cell phones and tablets running both iOS and Android. I strived to perform each repair to the best of my ability by using my analytical and problem-solving skills to ensure each repair was completed to a high standard of quality and reliability. I also served as the company’s primary computer repair technician during this time. This meant that I had the opportunity to take the same level of work integrity that I had employed fixing mobile devices to also service desktop and laptop computers running Windows and MacOS. The types of issues that these devices often had is more varied and, as such, it became even more valuable to have a keen problem-solving sense. This along with my other abilities as an organized and detail-oriented person have allowed me to succeed beyond expectations in this capacity. Due to my aforementioned success, I was also assigned several other tasks for the company. Firstly, I was assigned to update pricing information on our company website as well as within our internal systems every 3-6 months (when new prices were released). This process involved manually comparing new data with existing data to ensure that pricing was up to date and consistent across all platforms. I was also tasked with maintaining and updating internal computer systems across the company. My work in this area included resolving specific issues brought to my attention by other employees.

Software Developer/Architect

 December 2020 — May 2021

West-MEC

 My work for West-MEC consisted of a volunteer project to convert their cosmetology program’s record keeping system from an inefficient paper-based system to a highly organized electronic system. This required the system to be able to receive and store a large amount of data from students within the program and allow that data to be seen and processed by teachers. Additionally, this software had to be easily accessible, secure, and low cost. I was able to accomplish all of these tasks and more.

Multimedia Editor

 July 2016 — June 2018

RockHouseU

 In this role I worked as the primary editor on promotional video materials for the RockHouseU online music education platform. My day-to-day work involved editing assigned video footage remotely to a high degree of accuracy. The source footage was often shot on green screen and using multiple camera angles. This meant that I had to sync colors and audio across both sources so that the final product looked consistent and professional. Since the primary goal of these promotional materials was to demonstrate to prospective customers the quality and usability of the platform as a learning resource, it was immensely important for each video to reflect the quality present on the platform.
